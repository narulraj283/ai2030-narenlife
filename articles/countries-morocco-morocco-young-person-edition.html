As a young person in Morocco, you're entering a workforce of 12 million during the biggest technological shift since the internet. GDP per capita: $4,298. Average salaries: MAD 5,000 (~$500 USD). Tech roles: MAD 8,000-25,000/month; offshoring center salaries.
