About the Report

Lead the Shift is the most comprehensive collection of AI disruption scenario forecasts ever assembled. It contains 318 detailed analysis memos covering 70 countries, 142 companies, and 70 sectors — each written from multiple stakeholder perspectives including CEO, government, employee, consumer, and more.

Each article is structured as a memo from June 2030, providing a forward-looking analysis of how AI will reshape economies, industries, and societies by the end of the decade.
